MY DESIGNS

I am still just in my first semester of the Graphic Design program here at SAIT, but I wanted to share some of the work I have made so far.
They are actually designs I have come to incorporate into my "Opie Soaps" business. Enjoy!


I have also created designs (mainly posters) for events during the National Eating Disorder Awareness week in February for the last three years. This is a cause very near and dear to me as I, too, am in recovery and want to inspire hope for others. For anyone struggling, please reach out for help. An excellent Calgary-based organization is called the "Silver Linings Foundation" and is a wonderful resource for those in any stage of recovery.
